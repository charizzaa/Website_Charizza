Masterpieces

Latte

A latte is a popular coffee beverage that combines espresso with steamed milk, resulting in a smooth, creamy, and indulgent drink. It offers a harmonious balance of rich flavors and a velvety texture, making it a comforting and satisfying choice for coffee lovers.

Affogato

Affogato is a delightful Italian dessert that combines the richness of gelato or ice cream with the boldness of espresso. It is created by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of cold gelato, resulting in a heavenly contrast of temperatures and flavors. The hot espresso melts the gelato slightly, creating a creamy, indulgent treat that offers the perfect blend of coffee and dessert.

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee that packs a powerful punch of flavor. It is brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans at high pressure, resulting in a rich, bold, and intense beverage. The process extracts the coffee's aromatic oils and solubles, producing a small serving of strong coffee with a distinct crema on top.

Drip Coffee

Drip coffee is a classic and widely enjoyed method of brewing coffee. It involves a simple process of pouring hot water over coffee grounds held in a paper or metal filter. As the water drips through the grounds, it extracts the flavors, oils, and aromas, resulting in a flavorful and aromatic cup of coffee.

Espresso Based

Americano

An Americano is a popular coffee drink that combines the richness of espresso with hot water, resulting in a smooth and flavorful beverage. It is created by diluting one or more shots of espresso with an equal amount of hot water, creating a drink that closely resembles traditional brewed coffee but with a more intense flavor profile.

Cappucino

A cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee beverage that combines equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is known for its visually appealing presentation and balanced flavor profile.

Macchiato

A macchiato is a coffee beverage that combines the boldness of espresso with a small amount of milk, creating a drink with a strong and pronounced flavor. "Macchiato" means "stained" or "marked" in Italian, referring to the espresso shot being "stained" with a small amount of milk.

Mochaccino

A mochaccino, also known as a mocha, is a delightful coffee beverage that combines the flavors of espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk. It offers a harmonious blend of rich, bold coffee and indulgent chocolate, creating a satisfying and comforting treat.

Single Origin

Vietnamese Drip

Vietnamese drip coffee, also known as "ca phe sua da," is a unique and flavorful coffee brewing method that originates from Vietnam. It involves a slow drip process using a special metal filter called a "phin" to create a strong and aromatic coffee.

V60

The V60, also known as the Hario V60, is a popular pour-over coffee brewing method that allows for precise control over the brewing process, resulting in a clean and flavorful cup of coffee. Named after its distinct V-shaped cone design, the V60 is favored by coffee enthusiasts for its simplicity and ability to highlight the unique characteristics of different coffee beans.

Aeropress

The AeroPress is a popular and versatile coffee brewing device known for its simplicity and ability to produce a smooth and flavorful cup of coffee. It combines the principles of immersion brewing and gentle pressure to extract the best flavors from the coffee grounds.

Flat White

A flat white is a popular espresso-based coffee beverage that originated in Australia and New Zealand. It is characterized by its smooth and velvety texture, achieved through the careful balance of espresso and steamed milk.

Japanese Style Iced Coffee

Japanese-style iced coffee is a refreshing and flavorful way to enjoy coffee on hot days. This brewing method involves brewing hot coffee directly over ice, resulting in a chilled beverage that retains the vibrant flavors and aromas of freshly brewed coffee.
